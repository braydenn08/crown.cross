Refund Policy

At Crown & Cross, your satisfaction is our top priority. If you're not completely happy with your purchase, we’ll do our best to make it right.

Returns

We accept returns within 14 days of receiving your order. Items must be unused, unwashed, and in their original condition with all tags attached.

Non-Returnable Items

Custom designs, limited edition releases, or items marked “final sale” cannot be returned or exchanged unless defective or damaged.

Refunds

Once we receive your return and inspect the item, we’ll notify you of the approval or rejection of your refund. Approved refunds will be processed to your original payment method within 5–10 business days.

Exchanges

If you wish to exchange an item for a different size or color, please contact us first to confirm availability.

Shipping Costs

Shipping fees are non-refundable. You’ll be responsible for paying your own return shipping costs unless the item was defective or an error occurred on our part.

For all return and refund inquiries, please contact us here.
