About Crown & Cross

Crown & Cross began as an idea from Brayden Robert Asare, who wanted to create more than just clothing—he wanted to create a brand that carried both faith and style. Soon after, the vision grew into a team effort, as Judemunah Asamoah, Stephanie Faye Ankrah, and Nyhira Odame joined to bring the dream to life.

The name Crown & Cross was chosen to represent the powerful symbols of Jesus—the crown of victory and the cross of sacrifice. Our brand is rooted in Christian fashion, blending modern streetwear with timeless faith, so that every piece carries meaning and sparks conversations.

We believe that what you wear should reflect who you are. With Crown & Cross, you’re not just wearing clothes—you’re wearing a statement of faith, hope, and purpose.

“Wear Faith in Style.”
