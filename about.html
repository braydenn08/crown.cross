Our
Founders

Crown & Cross was founded in 2025 by Brayden Robert, Nhyria Odame and Eric with a vision to combine faith and fashion. Inspired by a desire to spread the message of Christ through modern streetwear, the brand was created to help believers express their faith confidently through what they wear.
